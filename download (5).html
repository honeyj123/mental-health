    About us

    Home About

    Addis Hiwot General Hospital is a private hospital located in the center of Addis Ababa, at Bole Sub-city Wereda 4. It was established in 2000G.C (1993E.C), by three Ethiopian physicians.

    The shareholders are physicians with high reputations and rich experience. They have been practicing medicine for over 30 years with different specialties namely; internal medicine, general and pediatrics surgery, Obstetrics, and gynecology.
